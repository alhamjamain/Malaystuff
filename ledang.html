Putri Gunung Ledang

A story about Sultan Melaka and Putri Gunung Ledang

This story started when Sultan Melaka sent a convoy led by Hang Tuah to propose to the Putri who lived at the top of Gunung Ledang. Putri knew that Sultan Melaka had many wives and concubines and this convoy was nothing but full of lust. Several times Hang Tuah and his convoy returned to the castle with empty hand. Sultan took this sign as a challenge that Putri was trying to play push and pull "tarik tali".


Thus, Sultan offered everything in his kingdom in order to get married with her. One day, Putri told the convoy that she will accept the proposal if Sultan fulfill these 7 sacred requirements.


  • 7 Dulang Hati Nyamuk
  • 7 Dulang Hati Kuman
  • 7 Dulang Air Mata Anak Dara
  • 7 Dulang Air Pinang Muda
  • 1 Jambatan Emas dari Gunung ledang ke Melaka
  • 1 Jambatan Perak dari Gunung ledang ke Melaka
  • 1 Mangkuk Darah Anak Lelaki Raja Melaka


Sultan Melaka was arrogant and and forced his subjects to do it. All virgins were gathered to cry for 7 days and 7 nights. All scholars were prohibited to go home, brainstorming how to gather "hati kuman and hati nyamuk". Any men in his kingdom must work building the bridges.Sultan Melaka did not care about his people well-being and pressuring to make it faster. Until the minister told Sultan Melaka about the last requirement;

1 Mangkuk Darah Anak Lelaki Raja Melaka
He fell onto the ground knowing he only had 1 son and the only crown prince. When Putri came and demanded the requirements to be presented, only 6 were present. Sultan told Putri that he did not have heart to murder his own son. So he asked the engagement to be canceled.

Moral of the Story

  • It is a reminder to the people that certain leaders are sellfish and not worthy to obey
  • Old people have better language understanding as rejection commonly came in hyperbole to keep the dignity of other parties
