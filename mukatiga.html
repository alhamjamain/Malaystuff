KERIS

KERIS

It is believed the word of Keris came from "kerik" which is a short form for an ancient Javanese language phrase "Malang Kerik" which means

"SENJATA AWAL HULUNYA BERUPA ORANG"

Keris Semenanjung

Mainly used in Singapore(Temasik), Southern Riau-Lingga and Northen Pattani The head or 'hulu' of Keris Semenanjung is called 'Hulu Jawa Demam' and certain has shaped of human body or flora concept.



The shape of sheath is square, wide

Keris Bali

In the area of Bali and Madura, the head of the Keris displays the God and Goddesses as Hindu is a common belief and religion. The head is typically made out of Gading and will be decorated with silver or any other colors or accecories.


For the sheath, mostly light color of wood with black strip will be choosen as the material

Keris Jawa

The special character of Keris Jawa is the head shaped like a pistol. Typically this model was used as heirloom during Javanesse empire. Famous heirloom Keris was made by Mpu Gandring.



There is a ring shape at the head of Keris called as Selut. The sheath is more on the thin side and it has dull end.
